What is a spirit animal?

In certain cultures, spirit animal refers to a spirit which helps guide or protect a person on a journey and whose characteristics that person shares or embodies. Can you choose what your spirit animal is? Spirit animals can help us feel more in tune with the earth and guide us to make the right decisions in life.
You cannot just choose a spirit animal, though; according to ancient beliefs, your spirit animal chooses you.
